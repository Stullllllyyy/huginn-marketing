Map Your Property

Every Buck. Every Camera.
Every Movement.

Drop pins, log trail cameras, and watch deer movement patterns emerge across your property. Huginn maps every sighting, tracks individual bucks across cameras, and shows you exactly where they're moving — and when.

  • Movement lines show chronological travel between cameras
  • Seasonal dot maps reveal activity clusters by month
  • Core area probability zones project bedding locations
Log What You See

AI That Learns
Your Bucks.

Upload a trail cam photo and Huginn's AI identifies the buck, matches it to your history, and auto-fills date, time, and weather. Every sighting builds a deeper profile — behavior, patterns, preferred conditions.

  • AI buck matching with confidence scoring and reasoning
  • Historical weather auto-applied to every sighting
  • Moon phase, wind direction, and behavior tracked automatically
Hunt With Intelligence

Your Property. Your Data.
Your Edge.

Huginn analyzes years of sighting data to tell you when to hunt, where to sit, and which bucks are moving. Daily hunt scores, AI-powered insights, and a property intelligence assistant that knows your land.

  • Daily hunt scores based on your property's actual deer behavior data
  • AI insights engine surfaces patterns you'd never find manually
  • Hunt Assistant answers questions about your specific property
